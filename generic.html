About

Irina Classic is a Russian professional classical pianist with an impressive history in modern music making. Irina is an Artist, Composer, Producer, Sound Engineer, Sound Designer. Having been born in a family influenced by music, Irina commenced her journey at the young age of 7, within the field of classical piano performing and composition in Russia, Krasnoyarsk. Her immense passion for music ultimately brought her to the "International Capital Of Music",LONDON, where she finished Point Blank Music College and did her Masters Degree in Audio Production (Commercial Music & Music For Films) at University Of Westminster where Irina developed her skills further and convert her classical background with modern technological and turned it into commercially viable product in the music industry. The classical background fused with the technical, programming, sound designing, mixing... , allow her to produce various genres of music such as modern electronic music with classical piano, RnB, House, Pop.
